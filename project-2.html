The Prop Master's Dream - Pop Up Exhibit

In our exhibition, we strived to unveil the multifaced identity of Wah Kwan Gwan, an introverted prop artist who throughout his life kept his true self a secret from those around him. Additionally, we aimed to shed light on the intricate world of Cantonese opera in Vancouver, an art form rish in diversity that is often misunderstood. Both subjects consist of several layers, and we showcased each of them through.
