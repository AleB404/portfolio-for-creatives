Taco Fusion - Brand Style Guide Project

Without a strong, meaningful personality, brands are forgettable and fade into obscurity. For a brand to be charismatic it has to have a voice people want to listen to and a story people want to hear. In this project students wrote the story for their restaurant and built the framework for a brand guide which will provide the foundation to develop compelling brand collateral.
